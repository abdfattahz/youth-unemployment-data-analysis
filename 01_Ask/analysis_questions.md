## Key Analysis Questions

- How have youth unemployment rates changed from **2016-2025** for:
  - ages **15-24**?
  - ages **15-30**?
- How did unemployment behave in three periods:
  - **pre-COVID**, **during COVID** and **post-COVID**?
- Which age group shows higher and more volatile unemployment over time?
- Have unemployment rates returned to pre-COVID levels, or do they remain elevated?
- What are the main patterns a decision-maker should know (peaks, declines, recovery)?
- What simple, data-backed recommendations follow from these trends
  (e.g. targeted support or monitoring for certain age groups)?
