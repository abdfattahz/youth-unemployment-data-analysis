## Primary Data Source

- **Provider:** Department of Statistics Malaysia (DOSM) - OpenDOSM
- **Topic:** Youth unemployment in Malaysia
- **Age groups:** 15-24, 15-30
- **Metrics:**
  - Unemployment rate (%)
  - Number of unemployed persons
- **Time range:** 2016 up to latest available data in 2025
- **Granularity:** Monthly, aggregated nationally (no state breakdown)

## Data Handling (Google Sheets)

- Downloaded as CSV from OpenDOSM.
- Opened in Google Sheets and checked for:
  - missing / blank values,
  - correct date and numeric formats.
- Added a `Year` column and basic formatting to support pivot tables and charts.
